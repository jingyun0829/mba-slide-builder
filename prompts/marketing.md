Marketing module emphasis:
- Consumer behavior, brand, pricing, channel — always anchored to a current brand students know.
- Prefer examples from the last 24 months: creator economy dynamics, AI-generated content, retail media networks, DTC economics, brand backlash cycles.
- Tensions worth planting: brand-building vs performance marketing, first-party data vs privacy, DTC unit economics, influence vs authenticity.
- Good closing moves: show a real campaign and ask students to diagnose what worked and what they'd change.
