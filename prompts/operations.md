Operations module emphasis:
- Process, capacity, supply chain, quality — always tied back to unit economics.
- Prefer examples from the last 24 months: near-shoring/friend-shoring shifts, AI in ops, semiconductor supply dynamics, logistics disruption, automation in services.
- Tensions worth planting: efficiency vs resilience, automation vs labor, lean vs buffer inventory, global vs regional footprint.
- Good closing moves: present a capacity or sourcing decision and force students to choose under uncertainty.
