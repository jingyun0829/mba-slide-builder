Strategy module emphasis:
- Name the framework (Porter, RBV, dynamic capabilities, platforms, ecosystems) but don't lecture it. Treat it as a lens.
- Prefer examples from the last 24 months: AI incumbents vs startups, platform regulation (DMA, antitrust), major M&A, vertical integration moves.
- Tensions worth planting: scale vs. differentiation, incumbent curse, regulatory capture, moat erosion from AI, build vs. buy.
- Good closing moves: ask students to predict a strategic move, then reveal what actually happened.
